Biography

Born in Ogun State, Nigeria, on January 22nd, 1991, Kadupe is a researcher and Ph.D. student with a keen interest in the sociology of music. His academic journey and professional endeavors are deeply rooted in the exploration of music's social dimensions, bridging interdisciplinary studies within the humanities.

Research Interests

Kadupe's primary research interest lies in the sociology of music. His work aims to understand the complex relationships between music, society, and culture, examining how music influences and is influenced by social structures and processes.
